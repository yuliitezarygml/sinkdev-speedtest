SPEED TEST

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Speed Test application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the speed testing service:

  • Network Information: IP address, ISP name, and network type (Wi-Fi/Mobile) to perform the test.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (based on IP) to select the optimal server for testing. We do not track your real-time GPS location.
  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version for diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Measuring your internet connection speed (Download, Upload, Ping, Jitter).
  • Displaying your test history within the app.
  • Improving the accuracy of server selection.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for core functionality:

  • Ookla / Speedtest.net: To provide the server network for testing.
  • IP-API: To identify your ISP and approximate location for server matching.
